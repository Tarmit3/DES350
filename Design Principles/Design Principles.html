Good Design Principles

1

Creativity

Brings design elements together with the expression of the designer without constrictions

2

Thought out

Thinking deeply about decisions made throughout the projects allows the designer to pay more attention to detail

3

Innovative

Moving forward with ideas and creating new ones with base foundation

4

Simple

Expressing art even without having many details

5

Understandable

Having a good path of knowing how the art works for the artist and viewer

6

Useful

Making sure the art is of use and has meaning

7

Beautiful

Having an art piece be specicial in its own expressive way

8

Unthinkable

Making the art stand out that no one else expects

9

Imaginative

Expressing art through the creativity of the mind

10

Bold

Making art pop through images or text being big or contrasting in color
